BIO

Get to know me

Hi!

I'm an actor, singer and songwriter, London-based but born and raised in South Italy.
After high school, I moved to the UK to train at London College of Music and graduate in BA Acting. From a very young age, acting has always been my no.1 aspiration, a different dimension where magic really happens (as dramatic as it can sound), and I've worked hard to pursue it.

On the other hand, music has always played an important role in my life. It was my very first challenge when my older sister kept telling me I couldn't sing (despite I was probably only 7). I picked up a guitar when I was 8 and impatiently learned how to play it.

Today writing songs is a necessity to organize my thoughts and share my own life experiences hoping to resonate with someone.
